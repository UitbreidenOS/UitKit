## When to activate

- Plan newsletter monetization — sponsor slots, paid tiers, affiliate integration
- Newsletter growth and revenue optimization

## When NOT to use

- For content writing (use newsletter-drafter)
- For social media strategy

## Instructions

1. **Audit current state.** Review subscriber count, open rates, revenue per subscriber.
2. **Identify channels.** Map acquisition channels and revenue opportunities.
3. **Design program.** Create incentive structure, pricing, or partnership framework.
4. **Implement.** Set up tracking, landing pages, automation flows.
5. **Optimize.** A/B test subject lines, CTAs, send times. Iterate on what works.

## Example

```
Strategy: monetization-strategist
Current metrics: 5,000 subscribers, 45% open rate
Target: 8,000 subscribers in 90 days
Tactics: Lead magnet + referral program + cross-promo
Projected result: +60% subscriber growth
```
