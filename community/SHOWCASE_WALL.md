- **Quality:** Improved deal sizing accuracy to 94% (vs. 72% manual estimates)

**Testimonial:**
> "Our sales engineering team went from spreadsheet warriors to strategic consultants. Claudient's agents handle the research grind so our team can focus on customer conversations. We're closing bigger deals, faster." — Marcus Chen, VP Sales Operations

## Submission Guidelines

### What We're Looking For

✓ **Real implementations** — Live deployments with measurable results  
✓ **Quantified impact** — Time saved, revenue, quality improvements, team size  
✓ **Domain depth** — Specialized use cases (not generic "we use Claude")  
✓ **Team stories** — From solo founders to enterprises; all scales welcome  
✓ **Replicable lessons** — Insights other teams can learn from  

### What We Don't Publish

✗ Unverified claims (no metrics or second-hand information)  
✗ Promotional content (thinly veiled product ads)  
✗ Vague case studies (generic praise without specifics)  
✗ Competitor showcases (if primary value is competitive advantage only)  

### Confidentiality & Privacy

- **Public by default:** Showcases are published with company name and team details (public profile)
- **Anonymous option:** If sensitive, request anonymization (e.g., "Enterprise FinTech Firm") — metrics and story still published
- **Review access:** Your PR reviewer may contact you to verify metrics (optional; trust-based process)
- **Rights:** You retain full rights to your story; Claudient may share in marketing materials with attribution

## FAQ

**Q: Do I have to share confidential metrics?**  
A: No. You can anonymize company name and round metrics (e.g., "40-60% faster" instead of exact % or absolute numbers). We value transparency but respect privacy.

**Q: Can we update our showcase later?**  
A: Yes. Submit a PR with updated metrics/details; we'll refresh the entry. Recommended: quarterly updates for active deployments.

**Q: What if we had a negative experience?**  
A: We welcome critical feedback via separate issue or discussion (not a showcase). Honest feedback helps us improve.

**Q: How do we request anonymity?**  
A: Add `"anonymous": true` to your submission. We'll publish the story with redacted company details (e.g., "Mid-market SaaS Startup").

**Q: Can we include testimonials from customers we serve (not internal staff)?**  
A: Yes, if relevant. A customer health insurance company sharing how your product improved their workflows is valid. We'll verify permissions.

**Q: Is there a minimum team size to showcase?**  
A: No. Indie hackers and solopreneurs are celebrated equally. Team size is informational, not a barrier to entry.
