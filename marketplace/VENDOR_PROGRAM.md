# Claudient Vendor Program

Welcome to the Claudient Marketplace Vendor Program — the official pathway for building and earning revenue from specialized stacks on Claude Code.

This document covers requirements, onboarding, support SLAs, revenue tiers, certification levels, and co-marketing opportunities.

---

## Program Overview

The Vendor Program is designed for professional stack authors who want to:

- **Build expertise** — Create domain-specific stacks using best practices
- **Earn revenue** — Monetize through subscriptions and support
- **Scale quickly** — Leverage the Claudient platform and community
- **Grow sustainably** — Access marketing, certification, and direct support

**Bottom line:** Build once. Ship to millions. Earn recurring revenue forever.

---

## Vendor Types

### Type 1: Community Vendor

**Best for:** Individual creators, small teams, open-source contributors

**Requirements:**
- 1+ published stack in marketplace
- Adheres to Publisher Guidelines (PUBLISHER_GUIDELINES.md)
- Maintains code of conduct alignment
- Responds to issues within 2 weeks

**Benefits:**
- Free hosting on Claudient Marketplace
- Community badge and profile page
- Access to vendor Discord community
- Community support via GitHub Issues
- Monthly newsletter feature (if qualified)

**No revenue share** — stacks are published freely under open-source licenses.

---

### Type 2: Professional Vendor

**Best for:** Consultants, agencies, teams offering paid stacks or support

**Requirements:**
- 2+ published stacks, OR 1 stack with 100+ installs in 60 days
- Meets Professional Vendor qualification criteria (see below)
- Formal vendor agreement signed
- Valid business entity (sole proprietor, LLC, corporation)
- Stripe or PayPal account for payouts
- Designated support contact (email / Slack)

**Benefits:**
- Professional vendor badge (marketplace + profile)
- Revenue share on paid stack subscriptions (70-80% split)
- Dedicated vendor support (email, Slack)
- Quarterly business reviews with marketplace team
- Marketing and co-promotion opportunities
- Early access to new marketplace features
- Vendor Summit invitations

**Revenue share:** 70% (first 6 months) → 80% (established vendors)

---

### Type 3: Enterprise Vendor

**Best for:** Large software companies, established consulting firms, agencies

**Requirements:**
- 3+ published stacks, OR sustained $10K+ monthly revenue
- Gold certification on at least one stack
- Dedicated team for maintenance and support
- Enterprise SLA commitment (24-48 hour response times)
- Custom integration or white-label requirements
- Formal enterprise agreement

**Benefits:**
- Enterprise vendor badge and dedicated marketplace profile
- Custom revenue terms (negotiable, typically 85-90% split)
- White-label stack support (publish under your brand)
- Priority support (Slack channel with dedicated Claudient PM)
- Joint marketing and co-branding opportunities
- Private SLA terms
- Exclusive featured placement on homepage
- Annual vendor review and strategy session

**Revenue share:** Custom (typically 85-90% split)

## Professional Vendor Qualification Criteria

To upgrade from Community to Professional Vendor status, your stack must meet:

### Adoption & Quality

| Metric | Requirement |
|--------|------------|
| **Installs (90 days)** | 100+ installs |
| **Average rating** | 4.0+ stars |
| **Active users (weekly)** | 50+ |
| **Critical issues (open)** | 0 unresolved for 2+ weeks |

### Maintenance

| Metric | Requirement |
|--------|------------|
| **Issue response time** | Within 1 week (all), 2 weeks (non-urgent) |
| **Last update** | Within 60 days |
| **Security patches** | Applied within 2 weeks of disclosure |
| **Dependency updates** | Tested and documented within 1 month |

### Support & Communication

| Metric | Requirement |
|--------|------------|
| **Support contact** | Dedicated email or Slack channel |
| **Support response** | Within 2 business days |
| **Issue resolution rate** | 80%+ of issues closed within 30 days |

## Onboarding Checklist

### Phase 1: Application (Week 1)

- [ ] **Prepare your stack(s)**
  - At least 1 stack published and gaining traction (50+ installs)
  - OR 2+ stacks published and maintained actively
  - Follows Claudient directory structure and guidelines
  - All content is properly licensed and attributed

- [ ] **Set up business entity** (if not already done)
  - Valid business name and entity type
  - EIN or equivalent tax ID
  - Legal business address

- [ ] **Configure payment**
  - Stripe Connect account (recommended) OR PayPal Business account
  - Bank account linked and verified
  - Tax documents ready (US: W-9 or equivalent)

- [ ] **Prepare vendor profile**
  - Author bio (200 words)
  - Website or GitHub profile URL
  - Logo or profile image (optional, 256x256 px)
  - Social media links (optional)
  - Preferred support contact (email + optional Slack username)

- [ ] **Submit application**
  - Email: [marketplace@claudient.dev](mailto:marketplace@claudient.dev)
  - Subject: "Professional Vendor Application: [Your Name]"
  - Attach: Stack URLs, business info, payment details, vendor profile
  - Include: Statement confirming compliance with Publisher Guidelines

### Phase 2: Review (Week 1–2)

- **Automated checks**
  - Repository validation (public, licensed, maintainable)
  - Marketplace metadata completeness
  - Install/rating/engagement metrics verification

- **Human review**
  - Stack quality assessment (completeness, accuracy, maintenance)
  - Author communication and professionalism
  - Business documentation review
  - Qualification criteria verification

- **Approval decision**
  - Accept → Vendor agreement sent
  - Request information → Follow-up email with questions
  - Defer → Resubmit after criteria improvements
  - Reject → Detailed feedback provided

### Phase 3: Agreement & Activation (Week 2–3)

- [ ] **Sign vendor agreement**
  - Review marketplace terms
  - Confirm revenue share structure
  - Agree to support SLAs
  - Accept marketplace policies

- [ ] **Complete onboarding call** (30 min)
  - Meet with marketplace team
  - Discuss stack positioning and marketing
  - Set up Slack channel (if applicable)
  - Confirm support process and escalation

- [ ] **Activate payment processing**
  - Verify Stripe/PayPal configuration
  - Test payout flow
  - Confirm bank account and tax documents

- [ ] **Launch Professional badge**
  - Vendor profile goes live
  - Professional badge appears on all stacks
  - Featured in "Professional Vendors" section
  - Newsletter announcement (optional)

**Timeline:** 3–4 weeks from application to activation

## Vendor Agreement & Legal

### Vendor Agreement Covers

1. **Rights & Ownership**
   - You retain all IP rights to your stack
   - You grant Claudient a perpetual, non-exclusive license to distribute
   - You represent that content is original and properly licensed

2. **Revenue Share**
   - Tier 1 (first 6 months): 70% to vendor, 30% to Claudient
   - Tier 2 (after 6 months, $1K+ earned): 80% to vendor, 20% to Claudient
   - Tier 3 (enterprise, negotiated): Custom split (typically 85-90%)
   - Payment: Monthly via Stripe, Claudient covers processing fees

3. **Support SLAs**
   - Professional: Respond to issues within 1 week, critical bugs within 2 weeks
   - Enterprise: 24-48 hour response time, 5-day resolution target for critical issues
   - Failure to meet SLA = marketplace team escalation or stack delist

4. **Maintenance Commitment**
   - Minimum monthly activity (updates, issue responses, or security patches)
   - Abandon stack without notice = delist after 6 months inactivity
   - Deprecated stacks: 60-day notice required + migration path provided

5. **Acceptable Use**
   - No harmful, illegal, or deceptive content
   - No bypassing Claude Code limitations
   - No spam, fake reviews, or manipulation
   - Violation = immediate removal and forfeiture of earnings

6. **Termination**
   - Either party can terminate with 30 days' notice
   - Existing subscriptions continue; you stop earning after notice date
   - Your stack remains in marketplace but marked as "legacy"

```
Day 1:  Application submitted
Day 3:  Initial review complete
Day 7:  Qualification decision + agreement sent
Day 14: Agreement signed + onboarding call scheduled
Day 21: Payment processing activated
Day 28: Professional badge live, subscriptions enabled
```

## Revenue Tiers & Payouts

### Revenue Split

#### Tier 1: New Professional Vendor (Months 0–6)
- **Your cut:** 70%
- **Claudient's cut:** 30%
- **Minimum subscription price:** $5/month or $50/year
- **Payout frequency:** Monthly (15th of month)
- **Minimum payout threshold:** $20

**Example:** 10 subscribers × $10/month = $100 revenue → $70 to you

#### Tier 2: Established Professional Vendor (6+ months + $1K earned)
- **Your cut:** 80%
- **Claudient's cut:** 20%
- **Auto-upgrade:** Automatic after meeting criteria
- **Request upgrade:** Email [marketplace@claudient.dev](mailto:marketplace@claudient.dev) with metrics

**Example:** 10 subscribers × $10/month = $100 revenue → $80 to you

#### Tier 3: Enterprise Vendor (Custom Terms)
- **Your cut:** 85–95% (negotiated)
- **Claudient's cut:** 5–15% (negotiated)
- **Volume discounts:** Available for 500+ monthly subscriptions
- **Dedicated PM:** Assigned for strategy and growth
- **Custom billing:** Annual contracts, net-30, prepayment options available

**Negotiation factors:**
- Annual revenue run-rate
- Exclusivity requirements
- Marketing investment from Claudient
- Co-development opportunities

### Payment Processing & Payouts

**Payment Method:** Stripe Connect (preferred) or PayPal

**Payout Schedule:**
- Calculated monthly (1st–30th of each month)
- Paid on the 15th of the following month
- Minimum threshold: $20 (holds accumulate until threshold met)
- Processing time: 2–5 business days to bank

**Example Timeline:**
- June 1–30: Earn $150
- July 15: $150 paid to bank (June earnings)
- July 1–31: Earn $200
- August 15: $200 paid to bank (July earnings)

**Payout Currency:** USD (Stripe handles conversions automatically)

**Payout Statement:**
- Real-time dashboard at `claudient.ai/vendor-dashboard`
- Detailed monthly statement (CSV export)
- Subscription count, MRR, churn, and lifetime revenue
- Tax-ready reporting (1099 equivalent)

### Tax & Legal Obligations

**You are responsible for:**
- Reporting income to tax authorities
- Paying self-employment or income tax
- Obtaining business licenses (if required in your jurisdiction)
- Maintaining records for accounting

**Claudient does NOT:**
- Withhold taxes
- File 1099 forms (vendor responsibility)
- Provide tax advice (consult a CPA)

**Claudient WILL:**
- Provide monthly earnings statements
- Issue annual revenue summary (if you request)
- Support Stripe tax forms (if applicable)

**For US vendors:** You will be asked to complete a W-9 or equivalent tax form during onboarding.

## Certification Levels

Vendors can pursue certification tiers to increase marketplace prominence and earn co-marketing benefits.

### Bronze Certification

**Criteria:**
- Stack passes all automated validation checks
- Completes human quality review with no blockers
- Author commits to 6-month maintenance

**Requirements to Maintain:**
- Minimum 10 installs
- 3.5+ average rating
- Zero unresolved critical issues
- Author responds to issues within 2 weeks

**Benefits:**
- Bronze certification badge (marketplace + profile)
- Listed in "Certified Stacks" section
- Priority in search results (above non-certified)
- Included in monthly featured rotations (rotation-based)
- Vendor listed in "Certified Vendors" directory

**Recertification:** 6 months from certification date

### Silver Certification

**Criteria:**
- Meets all Bronze requirements (initially)
- 50+ installs over 90 days
- 4.0+ average rating
- Active maintenance (updates minimum monthly)
- No critical issues outstanding >2 weeks

**Requirements to Maintain:**
- Minimum 50 installs (total)
- 4.0+ average rating
- Issue response within 1 week
- Critical bugs resolved within 2 weeks
- Quarterly updates (minimum)

**Benefits:**
- Silver certification badge (higher prominence)
- Featured in "Trending" and "Recommended" categories
- Co-marketing in monthly newsletter
- Eligibility for joint webinars/case studies
- Direct access to marketplace team (Slack channel)
- Optional: Core team co-maintenance offer

**Recertification:** 12 months from certification date

### Gold Certification

**Criteria:**
- Meets all Silver requirements (initially)
- 200+ installs over 180 days
- 4.5+ average rating
- Official maintainer endorsement
- Comprehensive documentation + examples
- Multi-language support (English + 1+ additional language)

**Requirements to Maintain:**
- Minimum 200 installs (total)
- 4.5+ average rating
- Issue response within 48 hours
- Critical bug resolution within 5 business days
- Monthly updates (minimum)
- Annual security audit

**Benefits:**
- Gold certification badge (highest prominence)
- Featured prominently on marketplace homepage
- Exclusive marketing support (co-authored guides, case studies)
- Featured in "Staff Picks" and curated lists
- Direct relationship with Claudient PM
- Joint event hosting (webinars, workshops)
- Revenue share eligibility (if not already active)
- Annual strategy session with marketplace leadership

**Recertification:** 24 months from certification date

### Certification Process

**Step 1: Automatic Evaluation** (Ongoing)
- Claudient tracks installs, ratings, issue response times, and maintenance

**Step 2: Application** (When ready)
- Email: [marketplace@claudient.dev](mailto:marketplace@claudient.dev)
- Subject: "Certification Application: [Stack Name]"
- Provide: Stack URL, metrics snapshot, maintenance confirmation

**Step 3: Review** (5–10 business days)
- Marketplace team evaluates against certification criteria
- May request additional documentation or examples

**Step 4: Approval & Activation** (1–2 weeks)
- Badge added to marketplace and vendor profile
- Stack listed in certification index
- Featured in curated lists (automatic rotation)
- Announcement in monthly vendor newsletter

### Decertification

Certification is immediately revoked if:

1. **Code of conduct violation** — Harmful, discriminatory, or unethical content discovered
2. **Critical security issue** — Unpatched vulnerability affecting user systems
3. **Licensing violation** — Use of incompatible, misattributed, or proprietary code
4. **Abandoned stack** — No author response for 3+ months after recertification review
5. **Hostile maintenance** — Author actively prevents improvements or ignores critical issues
6. **SLA violation** — Repeated failure to meet response/resolution targets

**Recovery:** Reapply 6 months after decertification, after addressing root cause.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Program & Eligibility

**Q: How much does it cost to join the vendor program?**  
A: Free. You only pay when you earn (revenue share split). No joining fees, no monthly dues.

**Q: Can community vendors make money?**  
A: Not through Claudient, but you can:
- Sell consulting or training around your stack
- Offer enterprise support packages
- Accept donations (GitHub Sponsors, Ko-fi)
- Use your stack as a portfolio for hiring/freelance work

**Q: How long does onboarding take?**  
A: 3–4 weeks from application to first subscription setup.

**Q: What if my stack is still in early stages?**  
A: Start as a Community Vendor. Once you reach 100+ installs and 4.0+ rating, apply for Professional status.

### Revenue & Payouts

**Q: When do I get paid?**  
A: Monthly on the 15th of the following month. Earnings accumulate until you hit $20 (minimum threshold).

**Q: How do refunds work?**  
A: Customers get a 30-day money-back guarantee. You refund via Stripe; Claudient doesn't mediate refunds.

**Q: What if a customer disputes a charge?**  
A: Stripe handles disputes. If a chargeback occurs, your earnings are held pending resolution.

**Q: Can I change my subscription price?**  
A: Yes. New price applies to new subscribers and renewals immediately. Existing subscribers keep their old price until renewal.

**Q: What happens if I stop maintaining my stack?**  
A: After 6 months of inactivity, the marketplace team will contact you. If still unresponsive, your stack will be delisted and you forfeit future earnings.

### Certification

**Q: How long does certification take?**  
A: 5–10 business days for review. Typically 2–3 weeks from application to badge activation.

**Q: Can I apply for multiple certifications?**  
A: Yes, each stack can be certified independently. If you have 3 stacks, each can be Bronze/Silver/Gold.

**Q: What if I lose certification?**  
A: You can reapply 6 months later after addressing the issue (e.g., fixing bugs, improving rating, resuming maintenance).

**Q: Does certification guarantee more sales?**  
A: No, but certified stacks get better visibility (featured placement, higher search ranking, newsletter features).

### Support & SLAs

**Q: What if I can't meet the SLA for a week?**  
A: Notify the marketplace team proactively. One-time exceptions are acceptable. Repeated failures trigger escalation.

**Q: Can I hire someone to help maintain my stack?**  
A: Yes. As long as responses meet SLA requirements, we don't care who provides them. List additional contacts in your vendor profile.

**Q: What if a critical bug is reported?**  
A: Professional vendors have 2 weeks to resolve; Enterprise have 5 business days. If you can't meet this, notify the team immediately for escalation.

### Co-Marketing

**Q: How do I get featured in the newsletter?**  
A: Maintain 4.0+ rating and request co-marketing. Marketplace team features ~3–4 stacks monthly (rotating).

**Q: Can I use Claudient branding in my marketing?**  
A: Yes, with permission. Use official logos (provided by marketplace team) and credit Claudient properly. No false endorsements.

**Q: Can I run paid ads for my stack?**  
A: Yes. State clearly that it's a paid ad (FTC requirement). Don't use Claudient branding without permission.

### Legal & Disputes

**Q: What if someone copies my stack?**  
A: If they violate your open-source license, contact the marketplace team. We handle DMCA and license violation reports.

**Q: Can I use proprietary code in my stack?**  
A: No. All content must be licensed under MIT, Apache 2.0, CC-BY-SA-4.0, or equivalent open-source license.

**Q: What if I want to sell white-label stacks?**  
A: This is an Enterprise Vendor feature. Contact [marketplace@claudient.dev](mailto:marketplace@claudient.dev) to discuss custom arrangements.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Build a Quality Stack

Create a stack that solves a real problem for a specific audience:

- **Minimum 10 skills** or a mix of skills + agents
- **Clear README** describing scope and use cases
- **Examples** showing real-world usage
- **Proper licensing** (MIT, Apache 2.0, or CC-BY-SA-4.0)
- **GitHub repository** (public, well-documented)

### Step 2: Publish to Marketplace

Follow the submission process in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md):

1. Prepare stack following Claudient structure
2. Write comprehensive README and STACK.json manifest
3. Open PR against `github.com/claudients/claudient`
4. Await review (typically 5–7 business days)
5. Once approved, stack appears in marketplace

### Step 3: Build Traction

Grow your stack to 100+ installs and 4.0+ rating:

- **Promote your stack** — Share in dev communities, social media, and your network
- **Update regularly** — Add new skills and address user feedback
- **Engage with users** — Respond to issues and feature requests
- **Gather feedback** — Ask users what would make it more useful

### Step 4: Apply for Professional Vendor Status

Once you reach 100+ installs and 4.0+ rating:

1. Email [marketplace@claudient.dev](mailto:marketplace@claudient.dev)
   - Subject: "Professional Vendor Application: [Your Name]"
   - Include: Stack URL, installation metrics, support contact, business info

2. Await review (5–7 business days)

3. Sign vendor agreement and activate payment processing

4. Celebrate — You're a Professional Vendor! 🎉

### Step 5 (Optional): Pursue Certification

Once you've maintained 4.0+ rating and monthly activity:

1. Email [marketplace@claudient.dev](mailto:marketplace@claudient.dev)
   - Subject: "Certification Application: [Stack Name]"
   - Include: Stack URL, metrics, maintenance confirmation

2. Await review (5–10 business days)

3. Badge activated and co-marketing opportunities unlocked
