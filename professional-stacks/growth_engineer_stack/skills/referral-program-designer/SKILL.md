## Instructions

1. **Assess current state.** Review existing metrics, channels, and conversion data.
2. **Identify opportunities.** Find highest-leverage growth levers based on data.
3. **Design experiment.** Define hypothesis, success metrics, sample size, duration.
4. **Implement.** Build the feature, flag, or campaign. Instrument tracking.
5. **Measure.** Analyze results against success criteria. Check guardrail metrics.
6. **Decide.** Ship, iterate, or kill based on evidence. Document learnings.
