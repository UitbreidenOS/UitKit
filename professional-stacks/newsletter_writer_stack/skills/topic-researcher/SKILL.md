# Topic Researcher Skill

## When to activate
User runs `/research-topic [topic]` or before drafting any newsletter to gather current data, trends, and expert insights.

## When NOT to use
This skill should not replace primary research when the user explicitly requests original analysis or investigation beyond publicly available sources.

## Instructions

1. **Search for current trends and data** on the given topic
   - Use WebSearch for news, recent publications, and trending insights
   - Look for data within the last 30–90 days
   - Identify 2–3 key data points or statistics

2. **Gather expert perspectives**
   - Find quotes, interviews, or commentary from recognized experts
   - Note their credentials and affiliation
   - Use WebFetch to extract full expert quotes when needed

3. **Identify competitive or alternative angles**
   - What are other perspectives or approaches?
   - What's the contrarian take?
   - Are there counterarguments or nuances?

4. **Compile research brief**
   - Topic summary (2–3 sentences)
   - 2–3 key trends or data points with sources
   - 1–2 expert quotes with attribution
   - Competitive/alternative angles
   - Suggested hook/angle for newsletter

5. **Surface to session log**
   - Log: topic researched, key data points, expert sources, angle selected
   - Flag any gaps or areas needing follow-up

## Example

**User:** `/research-topic AI regulation trends Q2 2026`

**Output:**
```
## AI Regulation Trends — Q2 2026 Research Brief

**Summary:** Global AI regulation accelerated in H1 2026 with EU AI Act full enforcement, US framework finalization, and emerging Asian standards.

**Key Data Points:**
1. EU AI Act penalties now up to 6% of global revenue (effective Jan 2026) — compliance costs rising for US tech firms
2. US AI Executive Order spawned 15+ agency-specific frameworks; implementation timelines staggered through 2027
3. Singapore, India, and UAE launched AI governance frameworks mirroring EU approach

**Expert Perspectives:**
- "The regulatory fragmentation is creating compliance burden" — [Expert Name], [Org]
- "Asia-first AI regulation is becoming table stakes for market entry" — [Expert Name], [Org]

**Alternative Angles:**
- How startups are navigating multi-jurisdiction compliance
- Which AI regulations are actually slowing innovation (and which aren't)
- The cost gap between large tech firms and SMBs complying

**Suggested Hook:** "Three regulatory frameworks now govern 80% of global AI deployment—here's how to track them."

**Next Step:** Ready to draft newsletter; recommend 2–3 sections on enforcement, compliance cost, and market impact.
```
