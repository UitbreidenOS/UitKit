## When to activate

- Evaluate and select product management tools — comparison matrices, migration planning
- Product operations and cross-functional coordination

## When NOT to use

- For product strategy (use roadmap-prioritizer)
- For user research (use user-research-synthesizer)

## Instructions

1. **Define scope.** Clarify objectives, stakeholders, and success criteria.
2. **Create plan.** Build timeline with milestones, owners, and dependencies.
3. **Coordinate.** Align teams, resolve blockers, track progress.
4. **Execute.** Follow through on deliverables, monitor quality.
5. **Review.** Collect feedback, document learnings, improve process.
