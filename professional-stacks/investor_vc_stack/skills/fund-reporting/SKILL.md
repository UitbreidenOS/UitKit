## When to activate

- Create LP updates, capital calls, and distribution notices for fund management
- Venture capital deal and fund management workflows

## When NOT to use

- For public market investing
- For personal financial planning

## Instructions

1. **Gather inputs.** Collect relevant data, documents, and financial information.
2. **Analyze.** Review terms, structures, and market comparables.
3. **Draft.** Generate document with standard formatting and legal language.
4. **Review.** Check for consistency, completeness, and compliance.
5. **Finalize.** Prepare for signature and distribution.
