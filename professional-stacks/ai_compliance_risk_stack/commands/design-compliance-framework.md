# Design Compliance Framework

## When to activate

When building or restructuring AI governance framework; establishing organizational policies for AI deployment; defining roles, responsibilities, and decision authority; aligning practices with regulatory requirements.

## Command Usage

```
/design-compliance-framework
```

## Inputs

- **Industry/Domain:** What sector? (Finance, Healthcare, Retail, Tech, etc.)
- **Regulatory Environment:** Which regulations apply? (GDPR, AI Act, HIPAA, CCPA, FCRA, EEOC, etc.)
- **Organizational Maturity:** Is this greenfield governance or enhancement of existing framework?
- **Scope:** How many AI systems? Model types? Deployment environments?

## Output

A complete compliance governance framework including:

1. **Regulatory Mapping** — Applicable regulations and requirements
2. **Governance Structure** — Roles, responsibilities, decision authority, escalation procedures
3. **Control Families** — 6-8 core control areas with specific procedures
4. **Policies** — Written governance policies (2-3 pages each)
5. **Templates** — Compliance checklist, risk register, model card, decision log
6. **Implementation Roadmap** — Timeline, resource requirements, dependencies
7. **Metrics & KPIs** — How to measure control effectiveness

## Example Outputs

- **Startup AI Governance Framework** — Lightweight framework for early-stage team
- **Enterprise AI Governance Framework** — Comprehensive framework for large organization
- **Industry-Specific Framework** — Finance, healthcare, employment, etc.
- **Regulatory Compliance Program** — GDPR, AI Act, HIPAA alignment
