# Team Building

## When to activate
- Scaling team from current headcount to next stage
- Designing organizational structure
- Planning culture and values framework
- Evaluating team gaps and hiring priorities

## When NOT to use
- Legal employment questions (consult HR lawyer)
- Compensation benchmarking (use external survey tools)
- Firing/termination decisions without full context

## Instructions
1. Map current team composition and gaps
2. Project headcount needs by function
3. Define org structure and reporting lines
4. Identify culture values and hiring criteria
5. Plan onboarding and team rituals

## Example
User asks: "We're 8 people, need to triple to 25. Where do we hire first?"
Skill outputs: Role priority order, reporting structure draft, culture framework, 6-month hiring plan
