# Investor Relations

## When to activate
- Preparing for board meetings or investor updates
- Communicating bad news or company performance dips
- Managing investor expectations or conflicts
- Planning investor communication cadence

## When NOT to use
- Legal disputes (use counsel)
- SEC reporting requirements (use compliance)

## Instructions
1. Clarify stakeholder group (board, investors, advisors)
2. Assess current sentiment and key concerns
3. Draft update or communication message
4. Identify metrics and narrative alignment
5. Schedule follow-up and action items

## Example
User asks: "We're 30% behind plan. How do we brief investors?"
Skill outputs: Communication narrative, accountability frame, corrective action plan, confidence signals
