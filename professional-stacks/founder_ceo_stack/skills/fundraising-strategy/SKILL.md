# Fundraising Strategy

## When to activate
- Preparing for investor pitch or round
- Evaluating term sheet or investor fit
- Designing cap table or equity strategy
- Planning fundraising timeline and targets

## When NOT to use
- Legal questions on financing structures (consult lawyer)
- Personal financial advice
- Evaluating specific investment opportunities beyond fit analysis

## Instructions
1. Clarify round stage (pre-seed, seed, Series A/B/C)
2. Identify investor profile (check size, stage focus, geography)
3. Map targeting strategy and messaging
4. Draft pitch outline or investor list
5. Surface key metrics investors will demand

## Example
User asks: "We're pre-seed, 6 months of runway, need Series A plan"
Skill outputs: Stage context, typical check sizes, key metrics to track, 6-month milestones before fundraising
