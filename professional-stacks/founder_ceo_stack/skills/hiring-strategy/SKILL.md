# Hiring Strategy

## When to activate
- Designing recruiting funnel and process
- Writing job descriptions and candidate criteria
- Planning compensation and equity packages
- Improving hiring velocity or quality

## When NOT to use
- Legal employment/contract questions
- Individual candidate evaluations (use interview notes)
- Termination or performance management decisions

## Instructions
1. Define role, criteria, and ideal candidate profile
2. Map recruiting channels and sourcing strategy
3. Design interview process and evaluation rubric
4. Set compensation and equity targets
5. Build recruiting timeline and velocity plan

## Example
User asks: "We need a VP Sales in 60 days. What's our recruiting plan?"
Skill outputs: Ideal candidate profile, sourcing channels, compensation range, interview process, 60-day timeline
