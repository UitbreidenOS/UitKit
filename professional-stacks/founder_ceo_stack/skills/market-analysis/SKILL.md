# Market Analysis

## When to activate
- Defining target market segments and sizing
- Competitive landscape research and positioning
- Go-to-market planning and channel selection
- Evaluating adjacency or expansion opportunities

## When NOT to use
- Detailed financial modeling (use financial-planning skill)
- Customer interviews or primary research analysis (use research approach)

## Instructions
1. Define addressable market (TAM/SAM/SOM)
2. Identify and analyze direct/indirect competitors
3. Map customer segments and value propositions
4. Analyze market trends and dynamics
5. Recommend positioning and go-to-market approach

## Example
User asks: "What's the real market size for our vertical?"
Skill outputs: TAM breakdown, segment analysis, competitor landscape, realistic SOM estimate, recommendation
