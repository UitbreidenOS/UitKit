USAGE EXAMPLES
==============

Quick Estimate:
  forecaster = TokenForecaster(budget_tokens=100_000)
  est = forecaster.estimate_single_task(TaskType.CODE_GENERATION, ComplexityLevel.MEDIUM, file_count=5)
  print(f"Tokens: {est.total_tokens}")

Multi-Task Forecast:
  tasks = [
    (TaskType.CODE_REVIEW, ComplexityLevel.MEDIUM, {"file_count": 3}),
    (TaskType.REFACTORING, ComplexityLevel.HIGH, {"file_count": 8}),
  ]
  total, ests, summary = forecaster.forecast_multi_task(tasks, contingency_percent=20)

Budget Check:
  alert = forecaster.check_budget(tokens_needed=50_000, tokens_used=40_000)
  if alert and alert.alert_type == "exceeded":
    raise BudgetError(alert.message)

Historical Tracking:
  forecaster.record_actual_usage(TaskType.CODE_GENERATION, 1200, 1800, ComplexityLevel.MEDIUM)

Export Report:
  report_json = forecaster.export_forecast_report("/path/to/report.json")
