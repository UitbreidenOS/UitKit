# Onboarding Optimizer

## When to activate

New hire offer accepted, start date confirmed, or first-day preparation underway. Triggers: offer acceptance confirmation, IT provisioning, manager onboarding kick-off.

## When NOT to use

Not for internal promotions (simplified 30-day plan). Not for contractors or temp hires. Not without access to role job description and manager expectations.

## Onboarding Framework

### Pre-Start (2 weeks before)
- Welcome email sent (first-day agenda, parking, IT check-in, manager intro)
- Equipment ordered (laptop, monitor, keyboard; delivered to home or office)
- System access provisioned (email, Slack, Github, project management tools)
- Mentor/buddy assigned (peer or senior IC; introduce via email)
- Org chart and team bios shared
- First 1:1 scheduled (manager, 30 min)
- Payroll/benefits enrollment completed

### Week 1: Immersion
- Day 1: IT check-in, welcome by manager, desk tour, team intros, HR compliance
- Day 2: Product demo, codebase overview, architecture review
- Day 3: Functional deep dive with mentor
- Day 4: Cross-functional intros
- Day 5: First project assignment (low-risk, 30-day goal)

### Month 1: Ramp & Context
Goals: Understand role, team dynamics, major projects, systems
- First project completion
- Documentation review/feedback
- Codebase/process walkthrough
- 1:1 with skip-level and peers
- Mentor feedback and manager check-in

### Month 2: Contribution & Autonomy
Goals: Move from observer to independent contributor
- Second project shipping
- Code/design reviews
- Mid-point manager review
- Team integration solid

### Month 3: Independence & Impact
Goals: Operate at expected performance level; contribute strategically
- Third project shipped independently
- 90-day formal review
- 360 feedback from team
- Strategic integration confirmed
