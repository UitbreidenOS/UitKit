# Culture Operations

## When to activate

Culture audit triggered, DEI initiative planned, recognition program launch, team health concern flagged, or culture misalignment identified in engagement survey.

## When NOT to use

Not for individual coaching or performance feedback (use performance-reviewer). Not for conflict resolution (escalate to mediation if needed). Not for policy decisions (use compliance-auditor).

## Culture Framework

### Culture Audit Dimensions
- **Values alignment:** Do employees understand and embrace company values?
- **Psychological safety:** Can employees speak up, share ideas, make mistakes without fear?
- **Inclusion & belonging:** Do underrepresented groups feel welcomed? Mentoring coverage?
- **Growth mindset:** Learning opportunities, feedback quality, career pathing visible?
- **Manager effectiveness:** Skip-level relationships, 1:1 quality, development conversations?
- **Work-life balance:** Burnout risk, flexible work adoption, PTO utilization rates?

### Recognition & Rewards Program
- **Formal recognition:** Monthly all-hands shout-outs, customer/peer recognition platform (e.g., Bonusly)
- **Compensation link:** Equity/bonus tied to culture values demonstrated
- **Peer-to-peer:** Bottom-up recognition encouraged; budget for small rewards ($25–100 range)
- **Milestone celebrations:** Work anniversaries, promotions, project completions
- **Transparency:** Share recognition stories; highlight culture exemplars

### DEI Strategy
- **Representation:** Track and publish demographic data (headcount, leadership, attrition by group)
- **Hiring:** Diverse interview slates (min 30%), inclusive job descriptions, recruiting partnerships with HBCU/women-focused orgs
- **Development:** Sponsorship programs for underrepresented talent; mentoring assignments; leadership development for women/minorities
- **Pay equity:** Annual audit; remediation for gaps >5%
- **Employee resource groups (ERGs):** Budget, sponsor support, connection/mentoring mission
- **Accountability:** Tie manager OKRs to diversity hiring/development; board-level reporting

### Team-Building & Culture Events
- **In-person:** Company offsite (annually), team happy hours (monthly), all-hands (monthly)
- **Virtual:** Lunch-and-learns, coffee chats, book club, wellness programs
- **Inclusive:** Offer virtual + in-person options; consider time zones; budget for accessibility
- **Feedback:** Post-event survey; adjust based on participation/feedback
