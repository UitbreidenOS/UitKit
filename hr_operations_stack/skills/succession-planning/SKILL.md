# Succession Planner

## When to activate

Annual leadership planning cycle, key role vacancy identified, critical talent at flight risk, or board succession planning triggered. Requires org chart, talent inventory, and performance/retention data.

## When NOT to use

Not for performance improvement plans (use performance-reviewer). Not without confidential talent data access. Not for individual career coaching (general development, not succession focus).

## Succession Planning Framework

### Critical Role Mapping
- **Tier 1 (CEO, COO, CFO, VP roles):** 100% coverage target; identify 2+ ready-now candidates for each
- **Tier 2 (Director, senior individual contributor roles):** 80% coverage target; 1–2 ready-soon candidates
- **Tier 3 (Manager, team lead roles):** 50% coverage target; identify emerging talent
- **Process:** Annual review; update quarterly or post-departure

### Bench Strength Assessment

**Ready-Now (Bench now ≥80% role fit):**
- Could step into role within 30 days with minimal onboarding
- Demonstrate performance at current level
- No gaps in critical skills
- Cultural fit confirmed by peers and skip-level

**Ready-Soon (Bench in 12 months, ≥70% role fit):**
- Clear development plan; 1–2 focus areas to master
- Mentoring relationship established
- High performance trajectory
- 12-month runway to full readiness

**High-Potential (Develop over 2–3 years):**
- Leadership ability and cultural fit evident
- Strong performance; learning agility
- Intentional development plan (stretch assignments, external learning)
- Tracked quarterly; re-evaluated annually

### Development Plan Template

For each ready-soon or high-potential candidate:
- **Current role & performance:** Baseline; recent review summary
- **Target role:** Position and timeline
- **Skill/experience gaps:** 2–3 focus areas; specific gaps
- **Development activities:** Stretch assignments (projects, interim leadership), external learning (courses, conferences), mentoring relationship (name, cadence)
- **Success metrics:** Milestones, feedback checkpoints, next review date
- **Timeline:** 12-month plan with quarterly check-ins
- **Contingency:** What if external hire necessary? What's the plan?

### Retention Strategy for Flight Risks
- **Identification:** Tenure + engagement score + market value analysis
- **Early intervention:** Manager conversation, growth discussion, compensation review
- **Options:** Promotion, expanded scope, equity refresh, role change, executive coaching
- **Escalation:** If at flight risk, flag for leadership and HR
- **Monitoring:** Quarterly touch-base for 12 months post-intervention

## Output Format

```
# Succession Plan

## Critical Roles Map
[Tier 1/2/3 roles; coverage status; ready-now bench count]

## Bench Strength Summary
[Ready-Now candidates by role, Ready-Soon candidates, High-Potential pipeline]

## Ready-Now Candidate Details
[Name, current role, readiness assessment, final onboarding needed]

## Ready-Soon Development Plans
[Name, target role, gap analysis, 12-month development plan, mentoring relationship]

## High-Potential Talent
[Name, target trajectory, current development, 2–3 year plan]

## Flight Risk Assessment
[Employees at risk, mitigation options, HR follow-up plan]

## Execution Timeline
[Next review cycle, development plan kick-off, feedback checkpoints]

## Contingency Plan
[If internal bench unavailable, external recruitment timeline and approach]
```
