# Performance Reviewer

## When to activate

Annual review cycle, mid-year check-in, 360-feedback collection window, or performance concern escalation. Requires employee file access, manager feedback, and peer/skip-level input.

## When NOT to use

Not for performance improvement plans (PIP). Not for disciplinary action. Not without documented prior feedback and opportunity to improve.

## Performance Review Framework

### SMART Goal Setting
- **Specific:** Clear, unambiguous outcome; measurable metric or deliverable
- **Measurable:** Quantified target (revenue, NPS, features shipped, retention rate)
- **Achievable:** Realistic within 12-month window; stretch goals acceptable
- **Relevant:** Aligned to company strategy and role expectations
- **Time-bound:** Clear deadline; quarterly milestones for 12-month goals

Example: "Improve onboarding NPS from 7.2 to 8.5 by Q4 2026 through documentation and mentor program redesign"

### 360-Feedback Collection
- **Respondent groups:** Direct manager, 2–3 peers, 1 skip-level, 1 direct report (if applicable)
- **Questions:** Strengths, growth areas, collaboration, communication, leadership (if applicable)
- **Anonymity:** Aggregate feedback; hide individual respondent identities
- **Synthesis:** Identify common themes; highlight outliers; contextualize for employee discussion

### Performance Rating Scale
- **Exceeds Expectations (E):** Consistently delivers above role scope; strong impact; high performance
- **Meets Expectations (M):** Solid execution of role; delivers on goals; reliable contributor
- **Below Expectations (B):** Misses goals or role expectations; performance gap identified; improvement plan recommended
- **Rating distribution targets:** E 10–15%, M 70–80%, B 5–10% (avoid clustering; flag outliers)

### Calibration Process
- **Panel calibration:** HR + department heads review all ratings; identify consistency issues
- **Outlier review:** Ratings above/below expected distribution; require justification
- **Bias check:** Ratings by gender, race, age, tenure; flag significant disparities
- **Legal hold:** Ensure all feedback documented; retain for 3+ years (compliance)

### Development Planning
- **Strengths to leverage:** How to apply top 2–3 strengths in next role or expanded scope?
- **Growth areas:** Top 2 development priorities; specific, actionable improvement steps
- **Career path:** Where is employee headed? What skills/experience needed?
- **Resources:** Training budget, mentorship, stretch assignments, external learning
- **Accountability:** Manager check-ins; quarterly progress updates; next review in 12 months

## Output Format

```
# Performance Review: [Employee Name]

## Goal Achievement Summary
[Goals set, achievement percentage, context for misses]

## 360 Feedback Synthesis
[Strengths themes, growth areas, peer feedback summary]

## Rating Justification
[Rating, calibration notes, comparison to peer group, legal documentation]

## Development Plan
[Top 2 growth areas, specific improvement steps, resources, timeline]

## Compensation Recommendation
[Merit increase %, equity refresh, promotion trajectory]

## Next Steps
[Manager follow-up cadence, development milestones, next review date]
```
