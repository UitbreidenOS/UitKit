# Shopify Operations Skill

## When to activate
- Writing or improving product descriptions for your Shopify store
- Figuring out when to reorder stock based on sales velocity
- Creating abandoned cart email sequences
- Responding to customer order status questions
- Analysing which products are performing and which aren't

## When NOT to use
- Building or coding your Shopify store (use a developer or Shopify's theme editor)
- Setting up Shopify apps — follow their official guides
- Processing refunds or disputes — do this directly in Shopify

## Instructions

### Write product descriptions that convert

```
Write a product description for:
Product: [name]
What it does: [brief description]
Key features: [list]
Who it's for: [target customer]
Price point: [$X]
Tone: [professional/fun/premium/casual]
```

Claude writes descriptions that:
- Lead with the benefit, not the feature
- Include natural keywords for SEO
- Match your brand voice
- End with a buying prompt

### Bulk product descriptions

```
I need descriptions for 10 products. Here are the names and brief notes on each:
1. [Product name] — [2-3 bullet points]
2. ...

Write them all in a consistent style. Tone: [describe your brand].
```

### Inventory reorder planning

```
Here are my last 3 months of Shopify sales data [paste or describe]:
- Product A: sold 45 units, current stock: 12
- Product B: sold 8 units, current stock: 30
- Product C: sold 120 units, current stock: 5

My lead time from supplier is 3 weeks.
Which products do I need to reorder now? How much of each?
```

### Demand forecasting

```
My Shopify store sells [type of products].
Last year's sales: [paste monthly totals if you have them]
Upcoming events/seasons: [Black Friday, Christmas, etc.]

What should I stock up on for [next season/quarter]?
What are the risks if I over- or under-order?
```

### Abandoned cart email sequence

```
I'm setting up abandoned cart emails in Shopify.
My store sells [type of products], average order value $[X].
Customers: [describe — local, age group, etc.]

Write a 3-email abandoned cart sequence:
- Email 1: 1 hour after abandonment
- Email 2: 24 hours after
- Email 3: 72 hours after (with a small discount)
```

### Customer order response templates

```
Write templates for these common customer enquiries for my Shopify store:
1. "Where is my order?" — it shipped, tracking attached
2. "Where is my order?" — it's delayed
3. "I want to return this" — within return window
4. "I want to return this" — outside return window
5. "I received the wrong item"

My return policy is [describe]. My tone is [friendly/professional].
```

### Sales performance analysis

```
Here's my Shopify data for last month [paste]:
Top sellers: ...
Slow movers: ...
New products: ...

What does this tell me about my inventory strategy?
Which products should I promote, discount, or discontinue?
```
