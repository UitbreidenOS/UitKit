# Skill: Expansion Opportunity

## When to activate

Quarterly business review or account health shows headroom for growth.

## When NOT to use

Do not recommend upsell if customer is at-risk or in onboarding phase.

## Instructions

1. Analyze current usage vs. plan limits
2. Identify underutilized features
3. Map to expansion offers (tier bump, add-on, seat)
4. Suggest outreach timing based on health

## Example

```
/expansion-scan --account=acme-corp
→ 85% seat utilization; recommend +10 seats
→ Best timing: post-renewal (Q2)
```
