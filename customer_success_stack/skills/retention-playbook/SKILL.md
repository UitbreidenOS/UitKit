# Skill: Retention Playbook

## When to activate

Account health <50 or explicit at-risk flag from churn detection.

## When NOT to use

Do not apply to new accounts in first 30 days (use onboarding instead).

## Instructions

1. Identify risk reason (low engagement, billing issue, support backlog, feature gap)
2. Select playbook (re-engagement cadence, executive touchbase, migration offer, etc.)
3. Schedule outreach with templated messaging
4. Track completion and sentiment response

## Example

```
/retention-playbook --account=startup-xyz --risk=engagement
→ Playbook: 3-week re-engagement series
→ Start: Tomorrow, 2pm PT
```
