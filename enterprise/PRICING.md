## FAQ

### How does Team billing work?
You pay per active seat per month. A seat is a unique user who signs in to the Team workspace in a given month. Add/remove users anytime — you're only charged for active seats.

### Can I upgrade anytime?
Yes. Upgrade from Community → Team / Team → Business / Business → Enterprise anytime. Billing adjusts prorated.

### Do you offer annual discounts?
Yes. Annual prepayment on Team/Business tiers gets 15% off. Contact sales for multi-year pricing.

### What happens if we downgrade?
Private stacks are archived but not deleted. Downgrade anytime with 30 days notice.

### Is there a free trial for Team / Business / Enterprise?
Yes. All teams get 14-day free trial of Team tier. No credit card required.

### What languages are supported?
Community, Team, Business: English (EN), French (FR), German (DE), Spanish (ES), Dutch (NL).
Enterprise: custom language packs available.

### How do governance hooks work?
Hooks log every time a skill is invoked: who ran it, when, on which file, and what was output. Useful for compliance audits and usage analytics.

### Can we use Claudient offline?
Community: Yes, skills are files in `~/.claude/`. Team+: Depends on subscription tier. Self-hosted Enterprise supports full offline mode.

### What's included in "Certified Marketplace"?
Verified, third-party stacks that have passed our vetting (security, quality, no bloat). You can publish your own stacks and earn revenue share (see [Marketplace Revenue Share](../marketplace/REVENUE_SHARE.md)).

### Who pays for enterprise SLAs?
Enterprise customers. 99.9% uptime SLA is guaranteed in contract. Downtime beyond agreed % results in service credits.
